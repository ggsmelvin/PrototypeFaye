Gloria (Patti Smith)
Patti Smith is the baddest bitch of all time. I found out about her through a book she wrote called "Just Kids" (which you need to read). She talks about her early 20's in New York City int he 60's and meeting this guy Robert Maplethorpe (In the 2nd pic down there) and how they fall in love and struggle as artists together and how she kind of makes her way from.. well, the bottom of the bottom.. to being a realized 'successful' artist. Again, she's the baddest of all time. Super dope authentic 60's hippie woman.
Say Yes (Elliot Smith)
If you haven't seen Good Will Hunting... I highly encourage you to do so lol. I just watched it a couple nights ago and this guy's music soundtracks the whole thing and it's MAGIC.
Clementine (Elliot Smith)
This song isn't in the movie.. But like, it's my favorite Elliot Smith song and it feels good to listen to after that last one lol. And I linked this interview cuz like.. ahh, it's just magic. He transforms the whole vibe of the room the second he starts playing.
Pumpkin (Been Stellar)
Been Stellar is a dope, NYC based indie band. They're signed to DirtyHit, which is an independent record label that Matty Healy from The 1975 started. He uses all the money his band makes to help cool, young artists. Bleachers, Beebadoobee, etc. are also signed to DirtyHit.. I didn't add this track to the playlist btw.. but the 2 on the playlist are my favorite off their album 'Scream From New York, NY'.. which is a no skips album.
Honey (Samia)
This girl opened for Bleachers when I saw them up north at The Great Saltair in like.. idk 2024 or something. Her last 2 albums are full no skip albums, you'll dig for sure.
You and Forever (Bleachers)
This is a volume as loud as it can go situation. And def don't watch the music video before listening to the song on it's own!! Bleachers are one of my favorite bands of all time... Jack Antonoff (who started Bleachers) was in this band called Fun that was huge when I was in high school and then he started this band and like.. ahh, he's just the best. I'm sure you know who he is. He produces like... Taylor Swift, Sabrina Carpenter, Kendrick Lamar.. like.. everyone's music these days lol. This is the music video which like.. it's cool, but the song just like.. is absolutely insane.
That Funny Feeling (Bo Burham)
I put the Phoebe Bridgers version on the playlist. But Bo Burnham actually wrote this song as a part of his Netflix special "Inside"... Which is like one of my favorite works of art. The whole thing is hilarious and clever. I think this guy is a genius lol and I'm like 90% he's dating Phoebe Bridgers..
Eighteen (Pale Waves)
I saved this for last cuz this song absolutely fucks. This band opened for The 1975 when I saw them in 2019 and like blew me away. I was in GA right up at the front and I swear to god, seeing this girl on stage felt like I was dreaming lol. This was the song they closed with I'm pretty sure... and honestly, it's what I remember the most even more than the actually 1975 concert itself lol. Ahhh, it's magic!! This vid was put out a month before I saw them live, so this is exactly what it felt like in person. Ahhhhh!! A dream, really.
